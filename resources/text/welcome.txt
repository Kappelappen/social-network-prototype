<h2>Welcome to SoulRoom</h2>

<p>
SoulRoom is a place to preserve the story of a life.
It is a digital space where memories, thoughts, photographs,
videos, and personal reflections can be gathered into a single
room that represents who you are and the journey you have taken.
</p>

<p>
For some, SoulRoom is a memory room for the soul.
For others, it is a living archive of a person's experiences,
relationships, dreams, and legacy.
Whatever your beliefs, every life leaves behind stories worth remembering.
</p>

<p>
During life, you can build your own SoulRoom,
adding chapters as your journey continues.
In time, it may also become a place where family and friends
can remember, reflect, and celebrate the moments
that shaped your life.
</p>

<p>
Every person has a unique story.
Every journey deserves to be remembered.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Welcome to SoulRoom.</strong>
</p>
